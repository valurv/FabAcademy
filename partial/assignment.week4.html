Week 4 electronics production

This week I make a FabISP board. The FabISP is an in-system programmer for AVR micro controllers, designed for production within FabLab. I will mill the board and then set some components on it. When I milled the board I had to use two sizes of milling bits. I used 1/64 milling bit when I was milling the trace and then I used 1/32 milling bit when cutting out the board.

Then it comes to the components. I started to set the usb connector on the board followed by the micro controller. Bit by bit I put the rest of the components on and ended with the 6 pin connector.
