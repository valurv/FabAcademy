Week 3 computer-controlled cutting

This week assignment is computer - controlled cutting. I started to find cardboard that I could use. Then I checked what setting would be best to cut the cardboard in the laser and started of course with the settings in the manual. They worked perfectly so I used them.

Then I made two parameters with different slots to find out what size of slot would be best for the cardboard that is 2,9 mm thick. The best size of slot was 2,7 mm.

I decided to cut a model of a TV cabinet that I was planning to build in week 8, make something big.

I made some mistake when I measured the perimeter, wherein the sides were longer than they were supposed to be, other than that the mission was successful.
